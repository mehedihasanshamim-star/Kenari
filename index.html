How Does
Kenari work?

Kenari guides students through the critical skills of English Language Arts, including reading comprehension, grammar, spelling, vocabulary, and composition.

Navigate through our many lessons yourself or simply sit back and let us guide you through a meaningful progression. We organize our content by grade level and keep everything self-paced so that students reinforce what they know while quickly progressing to challenging areas.
